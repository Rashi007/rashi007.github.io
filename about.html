About me

I also love designing the apps that I develop and if I am not babbling about coding techniques, architectures and clean code then I am surely neck deep into color palettes, logos and minimalistic UI.

I want to write my own book someday, and I have taken my first step in that direction by starting my blog based on tech, design and spirituality.

Now a days I am freelancing where I build amazing Android apps that users love and learning graphic design in my free time.
